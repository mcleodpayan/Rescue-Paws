Who We Are

Rescue Paws began with a simple belief: every animal deserves love, safety, and a second chance.
What started with a small group of passionate volunteers rescuing strays on the streets has grown into
a community-driven organization dedicated to saving lives and providing hope to animals in need.

Today, we collaborate with veterinarians, foster families, and local advocates to ensure that each rescue
receives medical care, nourishment, and the chance to find a loving, permanent home.
